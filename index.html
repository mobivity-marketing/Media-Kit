About Mobivity

Mobivity helps restaurant and retail brands grow their business by increasing customer frequency, engagement and spend. Mobivity's SmartSuite of products - including SmartReceipt™, SmartSMS, SmartAnalytics, and SmartScore - allow brands to unlock the power of customer, employee and POS data like never before. This creates a closed-loop marketing solution that provides SmartDATA-driven insights, attributions, and validation, at scale, to continually adapt and provide more personalized, relevant, localized and targeted customer communications.

About Mobivity's SmartSuite

Our SmartSuite of products unlocks valuable POS and mobile data to help transform customer transactions into actionable and attributable marketing insights. Our technology provides transactional data, in real-time, that uncovers market-basket information and attributes both online and traditional promotions.

SmartSMS

We’ve taken mobile marketing to the next level with our SmartSMS platform. The difference is in the results. With each SmartSMS campaign, clients can create smarter offers and drive higher redemption.

SmartReceipt™

This unique application unlocks the power of transactional data to create relevant and timely customer messages. Both clients and agencies are using SmartReceipt to drive better results and make decisions around offers, promotions, and customer engagement through the medium of the printed receipt.

SmartAnalytics

Mobivity has developed a POS-independent platform that allows brands, agencies, and franchisees to quickly and easily unlock key sales data, in real-time. Customers’ expectations are changing and marketing today means you have to be more relevant, more timely, and more aware of what’s working and what isn’t. Stop wait days or weeks to determine if your campaigns a working. Start using SmartAnalytics today.

SmartScore

Typically, marketing focuses its attention on engaging the customer and trying to change their buying behavior. At Mobivity, we believe including, and rewarding, employee behavior is key to effecting customer behavior and driving more revenue. By focusing on small changes - upsizing drinks, adding desserts, and promoting a LTO - employees can have a dramatic impact on sales.

Logo Usage

Mobivity's branding is primarily comprised of our wordmark. When you're using the logo with other graphic elements, please make sure you give our logo a little breathing room. Please follow the guidelines below when using our logo.

Respecting the Safe Area

When reproducing our logo, there should be safe space equal to the height of the letter m on all sides. It's important to keep all other imagery and text outside of this safe space.

In Living Color

While Mobivity Green will always be our favorite color, it doesn't work on all backgrounds. If green is not the best option, the Mobivity logo should always be used in our black or white.
