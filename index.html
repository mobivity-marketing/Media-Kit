About Mobivity

Mobivity provides a platform for intelligent and personalized marketing in the real world. Real world customer activity in national restaurants, retailers, and personal care brands is difficult to track and connect to digital actions. Mobivity leverages detailed purchase data and communications platforms to improve business results by understanding, predicting, and influencing consumer behavior. We drive better actions and inform decisions by connecting point of sale outcomes to the events and influences that caused them.

About Mobivity's SmartSuite

Mobivity's SmartSuite of products unlocks valuable POS and mobile data to help transform customer transactions into actionable and attributable marketing insights. Our technology provides transactional data, in real-time, that uncovers market-basket information and attributes both online and traditional promotions.

Download Platform Screenshots

SmartMessenger

Mobivity’s SmartMessenger platform transforms standard SMS messaging into a data-driven marketing medium. SmartMessenger tracks and measures offer effectiveness at a more granular level than anything available in the industry, allowing clients to create smarter offers and drive higher redemption rates.

SmartReceipt

SmartReceipt unlocks the power of transactional data to create relevant and timely customer messages. Both clients and agencies are using SmartReceipt to drive better results and make decisions around offers, promotions, and customer engagement through the medium of the printed receipt.

SmartAnalytics

SmartAnalytics is a POS-independent platform that allows brands, agencies, and franchisees to quickly and easily unlock key sales data, in real-time. Customers’ expectations are changing and marketing today means being more relevant, more timely, and more aware of what’s working and what isn’t. SmartAnalytics unlocks the transaction data gathered by SmartReceipt, and transforms it into actionable reporting from the basket level through full organizations.

SmartScore

Typically, marketing focuses its attention on engaging the customer and trying to change their buying behavior. SmartScore allows clients to begin including, and rewarding, employee behavior as a key method to effect customer behavior and drive more revenue. By focusing on small changes - upsizing drinks, adding desserts, and promoting limited time offers - employees can have a dramatic impact on sales.

Logo Usage

Mobivity's branding is primarily comprised of our wordmark. When you're using the logo with other graphic elements, please make sure you give our logo a little breathing room. Please follow the guidelines below when using our logo.

Respecting the Safe Area

When reproducing our logo, there should be safe space equal to the height of the letter m on all sides. It's important to keep all other imagery and text outside of this safe space.

In Living Color

While Mobivity Green will always be our favorite color, it doesn't work on all backgrounds. If green is not the best option, the Mobivity logo should always be used in our black or white.
